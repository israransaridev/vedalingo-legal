Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Vedalingo mobile application (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles your information. Your privacy is extremely important to us.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The Vedalingo app does not collect, store, share, or upload any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically, we do not collect:

  • ❌ Name
  • ❌ Email address
  • ❌ Phone number
  • ❌ Photos, videos, contacts
  • ❌ Location
  • ❌ Device identifiers
  • ❌ Login or account details

The app operates completely without user registration and does not ask for any personal data.

2. Translation Data

The App provides Hindi → Sanskrit and Sanskrit → Hindi translation features. The text entered by the user is used only to generate translations. We do not store, upload, or analyze your translation data on any server.

For convenience, your translation history is stored locally on your device only, using local storage. This data:

  • ✔ Remains on your device
  • ✔ Is not uploaded to any server
  • ✔ Can be deleted anytime by clearing app data

3. Internet Usage

The App requires an active internet connection to fetch translation results. Other than sending your entered text for translation processing, we do not transmit any additional information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services for monetization and translation purposes. These may collect some information as part of their own operations.

4.1 Google AdMob

We display Google Ads to support development. Google AdMob may automatically collect certain data such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • App usage data
  • Device information for ad personalization

This information is handled directly by Google under their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Translation API

The App uses an AI translation API to process text and return translation results. No personal data is shared—only the text you enter for translation is sent temporarily for processing.

5. Children’s Privacy

Vedalingo does not collect any personal information from children or adults. The App is safe for all age groups.

6. Data Security

Since Vedalingo does not store any personal data, the risk of data loss or misuse is minimal. Local translation history stored on your device is under your full control.

7. Links to Other Sites

The App does not contain external links other than advertising links provided through Google AdMob.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
